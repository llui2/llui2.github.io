[**Cycle holonomy induces higher-order constraints and controls remote synchronization transitions via twisted Laplacian spectra**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19682), arXiv preprint, April 2026.

Go around a loop and add the phase shifts. If they do not cancel, the loop changes how the network synchronizes.

# journal articles

[**Indirect influence on network diffusion**](https://doi.org/10.1038/s42005-025-02403-8), *Communications Physics*, Volume 8, Article 494, December 2025.

Let diffusion use paths of length two, three, or more. Those extra steps can change the slowest timescale.

[[arXiv:2505.05931](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.05931)] [[llui2/indirect-diffusion](https://github.com/llui2/indirect-diffusion)]


[**Structural prediction of super-diffusion in multiplex networks**](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.chaos.2024.115265), *Chaos, Solitons & Fractals*, Volume 186, September 2024, 115265.

Put two diffusion layers together and let nodes switch layers. The slowest mode can become faster than in either layer alone.

[[arXiv:2406.01367](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.01367)] [[llui2/super-diffusion](https://github.com/llui2/super-diffusion)]
